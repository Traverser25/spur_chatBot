{Overview}
Spur - Sell More. Support Better. Automate Everything
Spur is a multi-channel AI Agent for marketing and customer support. It helps businesses sell more, support better, and automate customer engagement across multiple channels including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and website chat.

{Core Offerings}
1. AI Live Chat: AI-powered customer support resolving up to 70% of queries instantly, context-aware natural language responses, escalates complex queries to human agents.
2. WhatsApp Business API: Automated order updates, abandoned cart recovery, personalized product recommendations; integrates with Razorpay, Stripe.
3. Instagram & Facebook Automation: Automates DMs and comment replies, qualifies leads and engages customers automatically.
4. Multi-Channel Inbox & CRM: Unified inbox, ticketing system, custom fields, team collaboration features.
5. Marketing Automation Suite: Bulk messaging to thousands of customers, event-triggered automated messages for abandoned carts, forms, etc.
6. Chat Widgets: Website integration for 24/7 AI chat, converts visitors into leads.

{Key Features}
- Automation of repetitive tasks and messaging.
- Comment-to-DM automation for social platforms.
- Lead capture and qualification workflows.
- Payment link generation and checkout automation.
- Multilingual support and easy no-code setup.
- Actionable AI integrates with external systems for real-time updates.
- Seamless handover to human agents when necessary.
